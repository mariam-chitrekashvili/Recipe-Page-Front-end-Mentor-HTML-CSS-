Simple Omlette Recipe

An easy and quick dish, perfect for any meal. This classic omelette combines beaten eggs cooked to perfection filled with your choice of cheese, vegetables, or meats.

Preparation time

  • Total: Approximately 10 minutes
  • Preparation: 5 minutes
  • Cooking: 5 minutes

Ingredients

  • 2-3 large eggs
  • Salt,to taste
  • Pepper, to taste
  • 1 tablespoon of butter or oil
  • Optional fillings:cheese. diced vegetables, cooked meats, herbs

Instructions

  1. Beat the eggs: In a bow, beat the eggs with pinch of salt and pepper until they are well mixed. You can add a tablspoon of water or milk for fluffier texture.
  2. Heat the pan:Place a non-stick fryig pan over medium heat and add butter or oil.
  3. Cook the Omlette: Once the butter is melted and dubbling. pout in the eggs. Tilt the pan to ensure the eggs evenly coat the surface.
  4. Add fillings (optional): When the eggs begin to set at the edges but are still slightly runny in the middle, sprinkle your chosen fillings over the one half of the omlette.
  5. Fold and serve: As the omlette continues to cook, carefully lift one edge and fold it ove the fillings. Let it cook for another minute, then slide it onto a plate.
  6. Enjoy: Serve hot, with additional salt and pepper if needed.
